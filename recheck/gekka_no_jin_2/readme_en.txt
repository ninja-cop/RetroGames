Gekka no Jin - The Siege under the Moon -

------------------------------------------------------------
■ Game Overview
------------------------------------------------------------

The year is 1274 A.D. on the island of Tsushima, shaken by the Mongol invasion.
As the lord of the island, you command your samurai to repel the encroaching Mongol army
by establishing strategic "Jin" (formations) and defending your homeland.

This game is a one-on-one strategic territory-control game based on the traditional
Japanese card game "Hanafuda." Skillfully utilize the monthly changing "Jin" to defeat the
enemy forces and restore peace to Tsushima.

------------------------------------------------------------
■ Controls
------------------------------------------------------------

This game is controlled entirely with the mouse.

- Select/Confirm Card: Left-click
- Use Buttons: Left-click

------------------------------------------------------------
■ Objective
------------------------------------------------------------

In each round (month), your goal is to fill the gauge at the center of the screen with your
color (blue). The first player to fill their gauge to the maximum (+100) wins the round.

The player who wins the most rounds out of a total of 12 will be the ultimate victor of the war.

------------------------------------------------------------
■ Basic Gameplay
------------------------------------------------------------

1. **Start of Turn**:
   At the beginning of your turn, one card is automatically drawn from the deck to your hand.

2. **Select a Card from Your Hand**:
   Click on a card from your hand that you wish to play.

3. **Place the Card on the Board**:
   Click on a slot on the board that matches the month (from 1 to 12) of your selected card.

4. **Scoring**:
   - If you place a card on an empty slot, you gain points equal to the card's base value.
   - If you place a card with a higher or equal rank on a slot occupied by an opponent's card,
     you capture their card and overwrite it with your own. In this case, the score added is
     "your card's points - the enemy card's points."
   - If your captured cards form a "Yaku" (a special combination), you earn additional bonus points.

5. **End of Turn**:
   After you place a card, the turn passes to your opponent.

Repeat these steps, and the first player to fill the gauge wins the round.

------------------------------------------------------------
■ Special Rules
------------------------------------------------------------

This game features special rules that add strategic depth to the battles.

**1. Yaku (Hand Combinations)**

By collecting specific combinations of cards, you can form a "Yaku," which grants powerful
effects or high scores.

- **Hand Yaku**:
  Activated by selecting a specific combination of cards from your hand and pressing the "YAKU" button.
  (Examples: Goko, Inoshikacho, Akatan, Aotan).
  When a Yaku is formed, the cards become yours, and you earn points. Some Yaku can grant
  an instant round win or allow you to steal an opponent's card.

- **Board Yaku**:
  If the total set of cards you have captured during play forms a Yaku, points are automatically added.

**2. Kasu Attack (Junk Card Attack)**

- Activated by selecting five "Kasu" (junk) cards from your hand and pressing the "ATTACK" button.
- After activation, you can target and destroy one card an opponent controls on the board.
- The owner of the destroyed card takes damage equal to twice the card's base points.
- This attack can only be used once per round by each player.

**3. Counter**

- Activated by selecting the November Kasu card ("Ono no Michikaze on a willow branch") from your hand
  and pressing the "COUNTER" button.
- Activation costs 15 gauge points.
- Once activated, you enter a counter state for 5 turns.
- If the opponent attempts to score points with a "Yaku" during this time, you nullify their effect
  and steal all the points from that Yaku for yourself.

**4. Jin (Formation)**

- Each round (e.g., January Jin, February Jin) has a designated "strongest card" for that month.
- If you play the corresponding "strongest card" for the current round on your turn, you activate a "Jin."
- While your "Jin" is active, all points you score are doubled.
- The "Jin" lasts until the opponent captures or destroys your "strongest card."
